How can you help prevent Climate Change?

Here is the most important task. Yes, you know about climate change, but how can you slow it down?
We started this, we end this. Below is a list of things YOU can do to help climate change.



    Reduce water

    Reducing your water can help to reduce carbon pollution. How? It takes energy to power your water. Yes, that tap you accidently left on for a long time not only makes your floor soaking, it uses up so much energy. You can help save water by turning the tap of when not needed and taking quick showers




    Carpool

    Do you know, that 28.8 of U.S greenhouse gas emissions are caused by transportation? Cars and buses cause so many emissions, and we don't need all that pollution in the air. Instead, what YOU can do, is carpool when you can with your friends. You can also take walkes instead of rides, or just bike to your destination. This was, you get stronger, AND you get more fresh air, that hopefully stays clean fresh because you chose to go green.



    Unplug

    Unpluging is important. Why? Because if you have a device thats fully charged and still pluged into a charger, thats nothing but wasting energy. Its all charged, it can't take any more. What can YOU do to save aall this energy? Yes. You can only plug in devices if they need to be charged, and remove them when done. You can also skip pluging in devices that you use less often, or you rarely use. This was, not only are you saving your electricity bills, you are also saving our planet. Good job!



    Buy the food you NEED

    When you buy food, make sure you eat it, for three reasons. Reason number 1, it is enviormentaly friendly. It takes a lot of energy to grow, process, package and ship all of your food. Reason number two, 40% of that shipped food ends up in the landfill. What a waste of energy! Reason number 3, a lot of people who don't have enough money to feed themselvesor their family... and to think 40% of that food ends up in the landfill! Only buy what you need, and please don't waste food.
