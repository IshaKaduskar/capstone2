Here is some more about Climate change


Climate Change threatens plants and animals, food supply, clean air and water. Climate Change, as you might have guessed, is not a joke. We have to do our best to stop it, because if we don't, it will be bad for not only amimals and plants, but for us.

Animals

More that a million animals are at risk from climate change, and could go extinct. This is not good, as we are the ones causing this. Scientists predict that by mid-century, 30 to 50% of all animals on earth will have gone extinct.

Can we reverse our doings?

By the way we are going, climate change is not stopping at all. By the year 2030, scientists say that some effects of climate change are going to be irreversible.

Government.

As you have probably known, a lot of world leaders are not taking climate change seriously. This is a major problem.

2019

2019 was the second warmestyear on record! As time moves on, climate change will still be here.
